About Me

From the streets and stands to the lens.

I'm Marwane Ait Bmn, a passionate photographer and visual designer based in Morocco. I am currently studying for my baccalaureate and I am a proud Wydadi.

Stadium Vibes

Red smoke, loud chants, and frozen moments.

Casablanca's stadium culture is a vibrant tapestry of passion and energy. I find endless inspiration in the raw atmosphere of big matches, the choreography of ultras groups, and the electric crowd dynamics.

  • Documenting ultras choreography and flares.
  • Capturing emotion before and after big matches.
  • Turning stadium chaos into clean, powerful frames.

All visuals are original photos and graphics. No official club logos are used.
